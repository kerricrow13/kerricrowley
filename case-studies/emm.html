FEATURED CASE STUDY

Educational Modality Mentor

An AI-powered decision support tool that helps educators select evidence-based learning strategies before building another module.

Role

Learning Strategist, Researcher, Designer

Timeline

2025–Present

Tools

ChatGPT, Canva, EBP Research

Overview

Educational requests often begin with a solution ("build a module") rather than a performance problem. I designed Educational Modality Mentor (EMM) to help educators identify evidence-based educational approaches before development begins.

The Challenge

During an evidence-based practice project, I found that instructional designers and educators frequently defaulted to eLearning regardless of the performance problem being addressed.

This led to unnecessary development effort and missed opportunities to use more effective modalities.

My Process

Research

Conducted an evidence-based practice review of educational modalities and behavior change.

Synthesis

Developed an Educational Modality Matrix that connected learning goals with modality recommendations.

AI Integration

Built a conversational assistant capable of recommending modalities and planning tools.

Lessons Learned
