CRRT: PrisMax Case Study

This immersive, unfolding case scenario simulates caring for a pediatric patient who requires CRRT (Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy) on the PrisMax machine. This 360° eLearning module assesses nursing competency on completing an environmental check, calculating fluid removal rates, monitoring labs while maintaining the CRRT circuit, and managing alarms. This project won the Best Poster Award at the 8th Annual CHOP Education Day Symposium. Tools used:

  • Articulate Storyline
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: Camtasia, SnagIt
  • AI Tools: Chat GPT, Copilot, Descript
  • Canva

Giovanni's Story: Navigating Pain Management and Sedation in the PICU

This interactive case-based course places learners in the role of a nurse caring for a 12-month-old patient with Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome (ARDS). Participants practice assessing pain and agitation, applying interventions, and documenting in Epic using validated tools like the State Behavioral Scale (SBS). The course reinforces clinical reasoning, interdisciplinary communication, and adherence to best practices in a high-acuity pediatric setting. It was created using rapid prototyping in 48 hours due to a regulatory need. Tools Used:

  • Articulate Rise
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: Camtasia, SnagIt
  • AI Tools: Chat GPT, Copilot, Descript
  • Canva
