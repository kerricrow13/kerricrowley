CRRT: PrisMax Case Study

This immersive, unfolding case scenario simulates caring for a pediatric patient who requires CRRT (Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy) on the PrisMax machine. This 360° eLearning module assesses nursing competency on completing an environmental check, calculating fluid removal rates, monitoring labs while maintaining the CRRT circuit, and managing alarms. This project won the Best Poster Award at the 8th Annual CHOP Education Day Symposium. Tools used:

  • Articulate Storyline
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: Camtasia, SnagIt
  • AI Tools: Chat GPT, Copilot, Descript
  • Canva

Giovanni's Story: Navigating Pain Management and Sedation in the PICU

This interactive case-based course places learners in the role of a nurse caring for a 12-month-old patient with Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome (ARDS). Participants practice assessing pain and agitation, applying interventions, and documenting in Epic using validated tools like the State Behavioral Scale (SBS). The course reinforces clinical reasoning, interdisciplinary communication, and adherence to best practices in a high-acuity pediatric setting. It was created using rapid prototyping in 48 hours due to a regulatory need. Tools Used:

  • Articulate Rise
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: Camtasia, SnagIt
  • AI Tools: Chat GPT, Copilot, Descript

Nursing Care of the Adolescent Patient with Suspected Substance Withdrawal

This eLearning module equips nurses to follow the inpatient clinical pathway for adolescents with suspected substance withdrawal. Learners practice CIWA-Ar and COWS assessments in Epic, review treatment and reassessment plans, and clarify nursing responsibilities in delivering safe, evidence-based care. Post-implementation results show improved confidence and accuracy in documentation, greater adherence to the pathway, and more timely treatment. Please review the educational outcomes data for more information. Tools used:

  • Articulate Rise, Articulate Storyline
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: Camtasia, SnagIt

Creating Accessible Digital Media: Small Modifications Make a Big Impact

I co-created this workshop with my colleague Carolyn Witt, CPACC. It aims to help professionals embed accessibility into every stage of content design. What began as a CHOP-based session has grown into a highly requested workshop presented at national and international conferences including ATD International Conference & Expo, NRG Summit in Germany, and the CLO Exchange for Healthcare & Life Sciences. Our curriculum focuses on practical, scalable strategies that show how small modifications can create more inclusive and engaging learning experiences. It is now a permanently offered professional development session at CHOP. We also published a blog called Designing for Inclusion: 6 Ways to Reach Everyone When You’re Presenting with ATD on this topic. Tools used:

  • Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • TechSmith Suite: SnagIt
  • AI Tools: Chat GPT, Hemingway, Readable
